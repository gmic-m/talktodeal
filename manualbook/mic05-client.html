MIC05

A customizable wearable voice hardware platform — engineered for ODM & OEM partners.

MIC05 is GIMC's reference wearable voice platform. Far-field capture, on-device DSP, configurable connectivity and enclosure — purpose-built to be re-shaped, re-skinned, and re-certified for AI hardware startups, enterprise SaaS extending into hardware, and industrial / field-service / healthcare device makers. Specs finalize at project scoping.

Wi-Fi · BLE · LTE On-device DSP IP-rated options OTA-ready

Used in field-service and voice-capture pilot deployments across field service, enterprise documentation, and industrial audio applications.
