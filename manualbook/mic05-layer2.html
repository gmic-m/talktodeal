§ 06 / SAMPLES
Sample Policy

Available upon NDA.

Policy · MIC05-SAMPLE

Sample units are available for qualified ODM partners upon signed NDA. Quantity and configuration are scoped per program — reference unit, partner-spec configuration, or pre-pilot lot.

  • Sample fee waived for qualified partners
  • Shipping arranged on partner account or quoted FOB Shenzhen
  • Sample units remain GIMC property unless purchase order is issued
  • Confidentiality and return / disposition terms specified in the NDA

Contact odm@gimc.example to initiate. NDA template available on request — see §08.

§ 07 / NRE
NRE & Tooling

Scoped at kickoff.

Terms · NRE / TOOLING

NRE (non-recurring engineering) and tooling cost are discussed at project scoping phase. Scope is set against the customization depth, certification footprint, and target volume.

  • Industrial design / mechanical engineering — scoped per enclosure
  • Firmware customization — DSP tuning, wake-word, OTA flow, button mapping
  • Tooling — soft tooling for pilot, hard tooling for mass production
  • Certification — test fees, lab time, re-cert per enclosure / antenna

Tooling ownership is negotiable — full transfer, shared, or factory-retained — scoped against volume commitment and enclosure investment level. Specifics in the program SOW.
